Your rights Terms

UbuntuFx is committed to protecting your privacy and handling personal information with transparency, responsibility, and care. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect information when you access the UbuntuFx website, platforms, tools, content, and services.

By using UbuntuFx, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of the Platform.

1) Scope of This Privacy Policy

This policy applies to:

  • The UbuntuFx website
  • Educational content, blogs, and learning resources
  • Newsletters and free downloads
  • Community platforms such as Telegram or social media (if applicable)
  • Future UbuntuFx web or mobile applications

This policy does not apply to third-party websites or services linked from UbuntuFx.

2) Information We Collect

UbuntuFx collects only the information necessary to operate and improve the educational platform.

2.1 Personal Information

We may collect limited personal information when you voluntarily provide it:

  • Email address (newsletter subscriptions or resource downloads)
  • Name or username (optional)
  • Contact information when communicating with us

UbuntuFx does not collect:

  • Financial account details
  • Trading account credentials
  • Payment card information (unless future services require it)

2.2 Non-Personal Information

We may automatically collect non-identifiable information including:

  • Browser and device type
  • IP address for analytics and security
  • Pages visited and interaction patterns
  • Referring URLs

This data helps improve platform performance and educational content.

3) How We Use Your Information

UbuntuFx uses collected information to:

  • Deliver newsletters, resources, and educational content
  • Respond to questions and communication requests
  • Improve site functionality and user experience
  • Monitor security and performance
  • Provide important announcements or updates

UbuntuFx does not use personal data for:

  • Selling or renting data to third parties
  • Aggressive marketing or targeted financial promotions
  • Automated trading decisions

4) Legal Basis for Processing Data

Personal data is processed based on:

  • Consent – when you voluntarily provide information
  • Legitimate interest – improving the platform and communication
  • Legal obligations – where required by applicable laws

You may withdraw consent at any time by contacting UbuntuFx.

5) Cookies and Tracking Technologies

UbuntuFx may use cookies to:

  • Improve navigation and usability
  • Understand user interaction with the site
  • Analyze traffic and platform performance

Cookies do not collect sensitive personal or financial information.

You can control or disable cookies through your browser settings, though some site features may be affected.

6) Data Sharing and Third Parties

UbuntuFx does not sell or trade personal information.

Limited data may be shared with:

  • Email delivery providers (for newsletters)
  • Analytics providers (site performance insights)
  • Hosting or infrastructure services

All third-party providers must handle data securely and use it only for the intended services.

7) Data Storage and Security

UbuntuFx applies reasonable safeguards including:

  • Secure servers and access controls
  • Restricted internal access to personal data
  • Regular platform maintenance and updates

However, no system can guarantee complete security. Use of the Platform is at your own risk.

8) Data Retention

Information is retained only as long as necessary to:

  • Provide requested services
  • Meet operational or legal requirements
  • Maintain communication records

When data is no longer required, it is securely deleted or anonymized.

9) Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent for communications
  • Unsubscribe from newsletters

To exercise these rights, contact:

Email: info@ubuntufx.com

10) International Users

UbuntuFx operates globally. By using the Platform, you acknowledge that information may be processed in jurisdictions outside your country, subject to reasonable data protection measures.

11) Children’s Privacy

UbuntuFx is not intended for individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal data from minors.

If a minor has provided personal information, please contact us and we will take appropriate action.

12) Third-Party Links

The Platform may contain links to external websites or tools.

UbuntuFx is not responsible for the privacy practices or content of third-party sites. Users should review the privacy policies of any external services they access.

13) Changes to This Privacy Policy

UbuntuFx may update this Privacy Policy periodically.

  • Updates will be posted on the Platform
  • The Effective Date will be revised
  • Continued use of the Platform indicates acceptance of the updated policy

14) Contact Information

Email: info@ubuntufx.com

15) Acknowledgment

By using UbuntuFx, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agreed to this Privacy Policy.

UbuntuFx remains committed to ethical data handling, transparency, and protecting the trust of its trading community.
