Who We Are

Structured, disciplined, education-first trading — built for long-term growth.

Clarity over hype

UbuntuFx was founded on 15 February 2019 with the goal of providing a structured, disciplined, and education-first approach to forex trading.

Built from real trading experience and a deep understanding of market structure, macroeconomic forces, and risk management, UbuntuFx exists to guide traders away from hype-driven decision-making and toward long-term growth.

Our approach emphasizes clarity, ethical standards, professional community conduct, and risk-first trading frameworks — creating a trusted environment where traders of all levels can learn, develop discipline, and grow with confidence.
