Sessions & timing

Know when the market actually moves.

Liquidity and volatility change by session. Understanding when institutions participate helps you avoid dead hours and trade with intention.

Tokyo Session

Typically steadier price action. Great for range behavior and early positioning before London volatility.

00:00–09:00 GMT

London Session

High liquidity and strong directional moves. Prime session for structure breaks and clean continuation setups.

New York Session
